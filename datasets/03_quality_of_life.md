# Top Cities Worldwide Quality of Life Index 2024

## Overview

The Top Cities Worldwide Quality of Life Index 2024 dataset provides a comparative analysis of the quality of life across major cities around the world. The dataset is compiled using data from Numbeo, one of the largest crowd-sourced databases for quality of life, cost of living, crime, healthcare, pollution, and transportation statistics.

This dataset serves as an important benchmark for evaluating urban development by measuring various aspects that contribute to the overall quality of life of a city's residents.

---

**Dataset Name:** Top Cities Worldwide Quality of Life Index 2024

## Dataset Statistics

- Total Cities: 150
- Total Features: 12
- Missing Values: None
- Data Quality: Clean

---

## Features

| Feature | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| Rank | Global ranking of the city |
| City | Name of the city |
| Country | Country where the city is located |
| Quality of Life Index | Overall quality of life score |
| Purchasing Power Index | Residents' purchasing power |
| Safety Index | Safety and crime indicator |
| Health Care Index | Quality of healthcare services |
| Cost of Living Index | Relative living expenses |
| Property Price to Income Ratio | Housing affordability |
| Traffic Commute Time Index | Average commuting time |
| Pollution Index | Air and environmental pollution |
| Climate Index | Climate comfort score |

## Why We Selected This Dataset

The dataset provides city-level indicators that directly measure the quality of urban living. These indicators complement socioeconomic, environmental, and infrastructure datasets used in our project.

The Quality of Life Index can also serve as a benchmark for evaluating the effectiveness of our City Development Index prediction model.
