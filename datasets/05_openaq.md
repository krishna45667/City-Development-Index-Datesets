# OpenAQ Air Quality Platform

## Overview

OpenAQ is a global open-data platform that aggregates air quality measurements from government agencies and environmental monitoring stations around the world. It provides standardized environmental data that can be used to assess pollution levels and urban sustainability.

Unlike a static dataset, OpenAQ is a continuously updated platform containing measurements from multiple cities and countries.

## Why We Selected OpenAQ

Environmental sustainability is an important component of city development. OpenAQ provides standardized air quality measurements collected from official monitoring stations worldwide.

These measurements help evaluate pollution levels and complement socioeconomic, infrastructure, and satellite datasets used in our project.

---

## Role in Our Project

OpenAQ will provide environmental indicators such as air pollution and atmospheric conditions that can be integrated with other datasets to improve City Development Index prediction.
