# OpenStreetMap (OSM)

## Overview

OpenStreetMap (OSM) is a collaborative, open-source geographic database that provides detailed information about roads, buildings, transportation networks, hospitals, schools, parks, and other urban infrastructure.

It is one of the largest openly available Geographic Information System (GIS) datasets and is widely used for urban planning, navigation, disaster management, and smart city research.

---

## Dataset Information

**Dataset Name:** OpenStreetMap

**Dataset Type:** Geographic Information System (GIS)

**Data Format:** XML (.osm), PBF, GeoJSON, Shapefile

## Why We Selected This Dataset

Urban infrastructure is one of the most important indicators of city development.

OpenStreetMap provides detailed geospatial information that allows us to quantify infrastructure availability and accessibility.

---

## Role in Our Project

This dataset will provide infrastructure-related features including:

- Road Density
- Hospital Distribution
- School Distribution
- Public Transport Availability
- Green Spaces
- Building Density

These features complement satellite imagery and socioeconomic indicators.
